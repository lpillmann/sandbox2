Features

With Traces, you can leave and discover media content connected to a concrete place.

  • Record a trace

    These could be messages, images, video, audio and even drawings!

  • Place it

    Your record will now be saved connected to that exact place!

  • Get it found

    Go ahead, surprise friends, family and even strangers that are present at that location, or will be!

  • Get recognized

    Get appreciated for your Hot Trace by earning Trace Points and become a Hot Tracer!

I care about this bench

Connect to people with the same interests.

Imagine living in Amsterdam. Amsterdam is full of beautiful places, places that need more recognition. Places that contain true art. We at Traces believe there is art in every place. Imagine for example a really beautiful bench. You go there every Sunday and just sit and enjoy the moment. You feel free. You would like to share this moment.

What about making a trace? Perhaps more people share this feeling! Your feeling could get acknowledged. You could connectto people that share the same feeling about the bench. And the bench gets a second life.

I am carving a heart

Leave and shape your moments

Think of all the moments we life through without properly realizing how unique the moments are. The small moments. The moments attached to beautiful places. Imagine for example that your lover visits you and you are walking on a beach during sunset. You are very much in love. While the beach may seem a normal place, at that point for you it is the creator of your special moment.

We at Traces believe those moments should be saved and shared with people that visit that place after you. Those places deserve to get recognized, the moments deserve to be shared. Friends, family or even strangers will get a happy surprise.

Just make a trace with your drawn heart in the sand of the beach. Or even better, draw the heart directly on the picture of the beach. With Traces, almost everything can be connected to your place!

I am getting a free coffee

Finding coupons for places right at your footstep.

Imagine walking to your office. It is a day like everyday; you know the route, you know the shortcuts and you definitely know your office location. However, what you might miss is that the coffee corner place on your way is considering to give away one free cup of coffee for a limited amount of customers. We at Traces believe that these location-based deals are often missed and therefore are inefficient.

With traces, the coffee corner place can instantly locate everyone around. Everyone passing by, including you, can find a trace with a coupon of free coffee. Value deals have never been so relevant for you!
